About This Project

The AI-Powered Loan Eligibility Advisor is a smart prediction system designed to simplify the loan approval process. It uses machine learning to analyze key financial details and provide fast, reliable loan eligibility predictions.

This tool helps users understand their chances of loan approval based on income, credit history, education level, employment status, and other important factors.

Our goal is to make financial decision-making easier, transparent, and accessible for everyone.
